Kai Zhang (张凯)

I am a master graduate student in Weill Cornell Graduate School of Medical Sciences at Cornell University, majoring in Biostatistics and Data Science. I obtained a BS degree with 1st class honor in Statistics from School of Science and Engineering at Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in 2019.

I am Graduate Research Assistant in Division of Biostatistics, Weill Cornell Medicine. I am working on two research projects simultaneously, under supervision of Prof.Xi Kathy Zhou and Prof.Arindam RoyChoudhury. One is focused on Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data analysis on mouse retinal pigment epithelia (RPE) and applies machine learning methods (Clustering) to identify subtype of PRE cells. The other one uses statistical methods (Baron and Kenny’s procedue based on Proportional Odds model) to provide evidence of mediation relationship among components of frailty. In 2018.07-2018.09, I took an internship at Changjiang Serurity.Co, serving a role of Credit Risk Analyst in Department of Risk Management in headquarter. My main work there was developing credit risk scorecards based on Logistics Regression model.
